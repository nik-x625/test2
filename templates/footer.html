About QLINES

QLines is a cloud-native platform designed to manage the remote devices, even when they are thousands of miles away, providing on-demand instant access. QLines allows easy monitoring of real-time sensor data via MQTT, HTTP, WebSockets,etc. The platform benefits from geo and local redundancy, providing a reliable high-performance data processing.
